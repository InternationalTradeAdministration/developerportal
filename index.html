    Data Services Platform

    Through this Trade Developer Portal, the International Trade Administration (ITA) gives software developers documentation and instructions for accessing ITAâ€™s Data Services platform. In turn, the Data Services platform provides direct access to authoritative information on U.S. exporting and international trade intended specifically for public use and dissemination.

    ITA has built the platform to be the central source for exporting data important to small and medium sized U.S. businesses. We encourage software developers to integrate this data into their applications and mash-ups to help U.S. small business owners expand their business overseas while creating jobs at home.

    Please review the list of Available APIs in the left hand navigation. We will continue to add APIs as they become available.
